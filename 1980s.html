The 1980s

High-Volume Nickel Production — Portrait Transition at Decade's End

Back to all decades
10Types Issued
99.9%Nickel
MachinPortrait 1980–89
1989Final Machin Year
The 1980s were a decade of steady, high-volume nickel quarter production with no commemorative interruptions — the standard caribou reverse reigned supreme for all ten years. Arnold Machin's tiara portrait graced every coin through 1989. The decade closed with Dora de Pedery-Hunt's new crowned portrait first appearing in 1990, making 1989 the last of the Machin years.
