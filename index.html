25-Cent Circulation Coins of the Elizabeth II Era — 1953 to 2022

During Queen Elizabeth II's nearly seven-decade reign, the Royal Canadian Mint struck 25-cent pieces every year from 1953 through 2022 (with only 1997 and 1998 excepted). The denomination saw four royal portraits, three metal compositions — from 80% silver through pure nickel to modern plated steel — and dozens of commemorative designs. This directory catalogues every circulation type, year by year, decade by decade.
