The 2010s

War of 1812 • Legendary Nature • Canada 150 • Coloured Coins

The 2010s produced a rich assortment of commemorative quarters alongside the annual caribou standard. Highlights include the War of 1812 hero series (2012–2013), the Legendary Nature coloured wildlife series (2011), Remembrance Day re-issues, and the Canada 150 celebration in 2017. All use Susanna Blunt's uncrowned portrait and multi-ply plated steel composition. Note: post-2017 mintage figures come from aggregated industry sources as the RCM stopped publishing annual struck-coin tables.

2010 Standard Caribou + WWII Anniversary

Standard annual issue. Blunt portrait, Hahn caribou reverse. Plated steel with RCM logo.

2010 — 65th Anniversary VE/VJ Day Comm.

2010Plated Steel

Commemorates the 65th anniversary of the end of World War II. Artist Cosme Saffioti — shows a soldier with rifle and maple leaf. Part of ongoing Remembrance series.

Mintage: 10,978,000
